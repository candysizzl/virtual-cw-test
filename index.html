FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is sourcing and vetting really free? +
Yes — there's no upfront cost for sourcing, screening, or shortlisting. You only pay once you've hired and your VA begins working.
How are VAs managed and monitored? +
Your VA works within your team like any remote employee. We use time-tracking software to ensure verified, productive hours — offering complete peace of mind.
Do I pay for sick days or public holidays? +
You only pay for hours worked. There's no obligation to cover sick days or Philippine public holidays, though you're welcome to if you wish.
What time zones do your VAs work in? +
Our VAs are experienced working across US, Australian, and UK business hours. We match you with candidates who align with your required time zone.
How quickly can I get started? +
Typically within 48–72 hours we present a shortlist. Full onboarding usually takes 1–2 weeks after you've selected your VA.
Can I hire a whole remote team? +
Absolutely. Many clients build entire remote teams through us — from VAs to developers to marketing specialists. There's no limit to how many you hire.
